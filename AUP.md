Acceptable Use Policy

## What's Allowed

- Normal conversations
- Work collaboration  
- Group chats
- Private discussions
- File sharing (within legal bounds)

## What's NOT Allowed

### Illegal Activities
- Drug trafficking or sales
- Weapons sales or trading
- Stolen goods marketplace
- Hacking or credential sharing
- Identity theft or fraud
- Child exploitation (zero tolerance)
- Human trafficking
- Terrorism coordination

### Harmful Content
- Threats of violence
- Harassment or stalking
- Doxxing or revenge porn
- Hate speech promoting violence
- Scams or phishing

## Enforcement

While we can't read encrypted messages, we will:

1. **Cooperate with Law Enforcement** - Legal requests will be honored
2. **Respond to Reports** - Credible reports investigated
3. **Terminate Service** - Shut down if abused
4. **Take Legal Action** - Pursue illegal use

## Your Responsibility

- Follow all applicable laws
- Don't share room codes with bad actors
- Report suspicious activity to authorities
- Use common sense

## Reporting

If you suspect illegal activity:
- Contact local law enforcement
- FBI: tips.fbi.gov
- NCMEC (child safety): cybertipline.org

## Disclaimer

We provide encryption tools, not immunity. Breaking the law in an encrypted chat is still breaking the law.

Don't be a criminal. It's that simple.
